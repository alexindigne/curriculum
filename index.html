About alexindigne

I’m Alejandro Indigne Acosta, a graphic designer with a creative and dynamic approach. With one year of experience in the field and an internship at a Dutch company, I’ve developed both technical skills and a global perspective. I’m proficient in tools like Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, After Effects, Premiere Pro, and Figma, allowing me to handle projects with a medium to advanced level of expertise.

I consider myself a highly creative individual, with a strong ability to generate innovative ideas and solve visual problems. My favorite area of focus in graphic design is typography, as I believe type has the unique power to convey messages in a distinctive and emotional way. I enjoy exploring new ways to combine and adapt typography to various styles. I also thrive in team settings, valuing collaboration as a way to enrich the projects I work on. I’m a native Spanish speaker with a B2 level in English, which allows me to effectively communicate in international environments.

My ability to work under pressure, attention to detail, and ease in adapting my designs to client needs are defining features of my work style. If you’re looking for a designer with a fresh perspective, strong technical skills, and a passion for typography, I’m ready to contribute to the success of your next project.
