About alexindigne

I am Alejandro Indigne Acosta, a passionate graphic design student with a focus on communication and marketing. My education is centered at Ciudad del Aprendiz, where I am developing strong skills in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign, in addition to notable experience in web design. Despite not having prior work experience in graphic design, I have showcased my talent by achieving the third place in a cover design competition for Neobis magazine and winning a diploma creation contest for Aules Transformadores.

My creative potential is high, allowing me to approach projects innovatively and effectively. Spanish is my native language, and I have a B2 level of English proficiency that enables me to communicate fluently in professional settings.

In addition to my technical skills, I excel as a highly creative individual with the ability to work in a team and communicate effectively. My sociable nature enables me to establish strong connections with colleagues and collaborators, which is essential in the field of graphic design.

I am committed to further developing my skills and building a strong portfolio as I progress in my graphic design education. I am actively seeking opportunities to learn and grow in this exciting field where my passion and creativity can flourish.
